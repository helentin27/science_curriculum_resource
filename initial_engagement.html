The Discovery of Life Science
Initial Engagement

One day, a child walked into the school with a small wire cage. In the cage, there was a tiny furry animal comfortably lying inside a plastic bowl. Three other children approached and one of them said, "Is it a hamster? It is very furry." Another child added, "Yes, it is very furry. Why? My froggy at home has no fur at all."

Children began to wonder and raise the question about animals' skin. That was the moment when their investigation started to expand.
